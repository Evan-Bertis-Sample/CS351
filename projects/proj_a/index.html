Project A: Moving, Joined 3D Assemblies

Evan Bertis-Sample

Enjoy this daring adventurer into a deeply immersive 3D world. Playing as the famous "buddy cube", gawk at the that tiny moon that orbits about a regular moon that orbits around nothing.
Use the WASD keys to move around and explore!

Planned Revisions

The cube is supposed to have legs attached. Currently, there are debug spheres representing the hips, knees, and feet (solved with IK). In the future, I plan to use the solved positions to place models of legs.
I am also planning on making a more sci-fi themed scene, and possibly make the render look more N64-esque (love that aesthetic)
